Part 2: Picking the Right Product

SoC vs Module: The Big Decision

ğŸ”² SoC (Chip Only)

  • Just the chip in QFN package
  • You add crystal, RF matching, antenna
  • You handle certification
  • Lowest unit cost at scale
  • Requires RF expertise

ğŸ“¦ Module (Ready to Use)

  • SoC + crystal + antenna + matching
  • Pre-certified (FCC, CE, etc.)
  • Faster time-to-market
  • No RF headaches
  • Best for volumes under 1M
ğŸ’¡
Our recommendation: Start with modules. Always. The pre-certification alone saves $10,000+ and 3-6 months.
